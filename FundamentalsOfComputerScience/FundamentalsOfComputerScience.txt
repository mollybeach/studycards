@ Introduction to Computers
Q) What is the history of computers?
A) The history of computers dates back to ancient times with the abacus. Modern computing began in the 20th century with the invention of electronic computers.
Q) Who is considered the father of computers?
A) Charles Babbage is considered the father of computers. He designed the Analytical Engine, which is considered the first mechanical computer.
Q) What was the first programmable computer?
A) The Z3, designed by Konrad Zuse in 1941, is considered the first programmable computer.
Q) What are the major milestones in computer history?
A) Major milestones include the invention of the transistor in 1947, the development of integrated circuits in the 1950s, and the creation of the personal computer in the 1970s and 1980s.
Q) How has computer technology evolved over time?
A) Computer technology has evolved from mechanical and vacuum tube-based systems to transistors and integrated circuits, leading to powerful and compact modern computers.
